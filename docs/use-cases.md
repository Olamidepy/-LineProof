# Use Cases

LineProof solves fairness problems across multiple industries. This document explores real-world applications.

## Healthcare Scheduling

### Problem
Medical appointments, especially specialists and surgeries, often have waitlists where:
- Patients cannot verify their position is legitimate
- Administrative staff may show favoritism
- No recourse if appointments are cancelled after long waits

### LineProof Solution
- Patients enroll with identity-bound positions
- Queue advancement follows published criteria (urgency, appointment availability)
- Full audit trail enables regulatory oversight
- Escrow can hold co-pay deposits released upon service

### Benefits
- Transparent wait time communication
- Reduction in administrative burden
- Compliance with healthcare fairness regulations

## Visa Appointment Systems

### Problem
Government visa systems face:
- Queue jumping through insider connections
- Scalping of appointment slots
- No public accountability for wait times

### LineProof Solution
- One appointment per passport/identity
- Public queue progression with on-chain proof
- Non-transferable slots prevent resale
- Audit logs support diplomatic transparency efforts

### Benefits
- Reduced corruption opportunities
- Predictable appointment scheduling
- Enables cross-border fairness verification

## University Admissions

### Problem
Selective university admissions often lack transparency:
- Students cannot verify selection criteria were applied equally
- Waitlists may favor certain applicants
- No public accountability for admission decisions

### LineProof Solution
- Applicant identity binding with document verification
- Configurable advancement rules (GPA thresholds, lottery, priority tiers)
- Immutable audit trail of all admission actions
- Optional escrow for application fees

### Benefits
- Increased trust in admission processes
- Reduced litigation from rejected applicants
- Transparent waitlist management

## Event Ticketing

### Problem
Concert and event tickets suffer from:
- Bot scalping of high-demand events
- Secondary market markup
- No fairness guarantee for true fans

### LineProof Solution
- Non-transferable ticket claims
- Identity-bound entry at venue
- Optional refundable deposits to reduce no-shows
- Deterministic allocation (FIFO or VRF lottery)

### Benefits
- Fan-friendly ticketing experience
- Reduced secondary market manipulation
- Venue can verify legitimate ticket holders

## Product Launches

### Problem
Limited-edition product drops face:
- Automated bot purchasing
- Instant resale at markup
- Inequitable distribution

### LineProof Solution
- Identity-gated purchase queues
- Non-transferable claims
- Escrow holds payment until shipping confirmation
- Proof of legitimate purchase for authentication

### Benefits
- Fair distribution to genuine customers
- Reduced bot infrastructure costs
- Improved customer trust
